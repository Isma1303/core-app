# Master Design Styles: Notion, Apple, Uber

This skill equips the agent with three distinct professional minimalist aesthetics: **Notion**, **Apple**, and **Uber**. Use these to deliver "wowed" first impressions with a "Less is More" philosophy.

## 1. Notion Style (Warm Minimalist)
- **Aesthetic**: approachable, analog warmth, high-quality paper feel.
- **Colors**: Background `#FFFFFF`, Text `rgba(0,0,0,0.95)`, Warm White `#F6F5F4` for section alternation.
- **Borders**: Whisper-weight `1px solid rgba(0,0,0,0.1)`.
- **Shadows**: Multi-layer stacks with total opacity < 0.05.
- **Typography**: Inter with negative letter-spacing (-2.125px at 64px).
- **Accent**: Notion Blue `#0075DE`.

## 2. Apple Style (Cinematic Minimalist)
- **Aesthetic**: Cinematic drama, product-as-hero, Invisible UI.
- **Colors**: Pure Black (#000000) vs Light Gray (#F5F5F7). High contrast.
- **Typography**: SF Pro (or Inter). Headlines very tight (line-height 1.07).
- **Accent**: Apple Blue `#0071E3` exclusively for interactive elements.
- **Glassmorphism**: Translucent dark nav (`rgba(0,0,0,0.8)` + 20px blur).
- **Radius**: 8px-12px for cards, 980px (pill) for CTAs.

## 3. Uber Style (Geometric Minimalist)
- **Aesthetic**: Engineered efficiency, bold billboard presence, zero gradients.
- **Colors**: Purest Black (#000000) and Purest White (#FFFFFF). No mid-grays in UI.
- **Typography**: Bold (700) headlines, Medium (500) UI text. Geometric sans-serif.
- **Radius**: 999px (full pill) for ALL interactive elements (buttons, chips).
- **Shadows**: Whisper-soft `rgba(0,0,0,0.12)`.
